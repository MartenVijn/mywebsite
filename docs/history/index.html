A brief History...

home

I was born in Almelo at the beginning 1967. So that’s a long long time ago.

As a child we moved a lot, living at several places. I have old older brother and younger sister. In the mid 70’s we moved to the Leiden regio in the Netherlands, where I still live.

I’ve done most of school and education here. My latest grades are for physical and occupational therapy.

Then, a tat late some would say, I started to play with tech stuff, computers and networks. Evolving projects like Wireless Leiden, Open Community Camp and most late, MakerSpace Leiden.

From a retrospective, I ‘ve alway been involved with tech and people; getting things organized and realized. And also, there is a long list of unfished or dead projects; there are a of things that pass my buzy mind.

Over the last couple of years I tend to move a bit away from tech, exploring other sides of humanity.
